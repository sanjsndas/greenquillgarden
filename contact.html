Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I schedule a garden consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, using the contact form on this page, or visiting our showroom during business hours. Our team will work with you to find a convenient time for an on-site visit or virtual consultation. We recommend scheduling at least two weeks in advance during peak seasons.

2. What information should I provide when requesting a quote?

For accurate quotes, please provide details about your property size, existing landscape features, specific plants or materials you're interested in, and any special requirements. Photos of your space are extremely helpful. Our designers may request a site visit for complex projects to ensure the quote reflects all aspects of your vision.

3. How long does the design process typically take?

The design process varies based on project complexity. Simple consultations may be completed in one visit, while comprehensive designs for large properties typically take 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to final plan delivery. We'll provide a timeline estimate after our initial meeting and keep you updated throughout the process.

4. Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for clients who prefer remote services or are located outside our immediate service area. These sessions include the same expert advice as in-person visits, with follow-up materials sent digitally. You'll need to provide photos and measurements of your space beforehand for the most productive consultation.

5. What are your payment terms for design services?

We require a 50% deposit to begin design work, with the balance due upon delivery of final plans. For large projects, we may offer installment payments. We accept all major credit cards, checks, and bank transfers. Detailed invoices are provided for all services, and we're happy to discuss payment plans for budget-conscious clients.

6. How do I care for plants purchased from your nursery?

All plants purchased from our nursery come with detailed care instructions specific to each species. Our horticulturists are also available for follow-up questions. We recommend scheduling a post-purchase consultation to ensure your plants thrive, especially if you're new to gardening or have challenging growing conditions.

7. What's your policy on plant replacements or guarantees?

We offer a 90-day guarantee on most plants when proper care instructions are followed. If a plant fails to thrive due to nursery-related issues, we'll replace it at no cost. Please bring the plant (or clear photos) and your receipt to our nursery for evaluation. This guarantee doesn't cover damage from extreme weather, pests, or improper care.

8. Can you work within my homeowners association (HOA) guidelines?

Absolutely. Our designers are experienced in creating landscapes that meet HOA requirements while reflecting your personal style. Please provide your HOA's landscaping guidelines during our initial consultation. We'll handle all necessary submissions and revisions to ensure your project complies while achieving your vision.

9. Do you provide maintenance services after installation?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance services to ensure your garden remains healthy and beautiful. Our maintenance packages can be customized to fit your needs, whether you require weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits. We can handle everything from lawn care to seasonal plantings, ensuring your landscape thrives year-round.

10. What types of plants do you recommend for my area?

Our team is knowledgeable about local climate conditions and soil types, allowing us to recommend plants that will thrive in your specific environment. We focus on native and drought-resistant species that require less maintenance and are more resilient to local pests and diseases. During your consultation, we can discuss the best options for your garden.

11. How can I prepare my garden for winter?

Preparing your garden for winter involves several steps, including mulching, pruning, and protecting sensitive plants. We recommend scheduling a winter preparation consultation where our experts can provide tailored advice based on your garden's specific needs. This ensures your plants are well-protected and ready to thrive in the spring.

12. Can you help with landscape design for commercial properties?

Yes, we specialize in landscape design for both residential and commercial properties. Our team understands the unique requirements of commercial spaces and can create designs that enhance curb appeal, comply with zoning regulations, and provide functional outdoor areas for employees and customers. Contact us to discuss your project.

13. What should I do if I have a pest problem in my garden?

If you suspect a pest problem, it's important to act quickly. We recommend contacting our team for a pest assessment. Our experts can identify the issue and recommend appropriate treatments, whether organic or chemical, to protect your plants. Regular maintenance visits can also help prevent pest issues before they arise.

14. Do you offer workshops or classes on gardening?

Yes, we offer a variety of workshops and classes throughout the year, covering topics such as plant care, landscape design, and sustainable gardening practices. These sessions are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced gardeners. Check our website for upcoming events and registration details.

15. How do I choose the right mulch for my garden?

Choosing the right mulch depends on your garden's needs and aesthetic preferences. We can help you select from various options, including organic mulches like wood chips and straw, or inorganic options like gravel and rubber. Each type has its benefits, such as moisture retention, weed suppression, and visual appeal. Schedule a consultation for personalized advice.

16. What are the benefits of native plants?

Native plants are adapted to local conditions, making them more resilient to pests and diseases. They require less water and maintenance compared to non-native species, and they support local wildlife, including pollinators. Incorporating native plants into your landscape can enhance biodiversity and create a sustainable garden environment.

17. How can I improve soil quality in my garden?

Improving soil quality involves testing your soil, adding organic matter, and ensuring proper drainage. We recommend conducting a soil test to determine nutrient levels and pH. Based on the results, we can suggest amendments such as compost, fertilizers, or soil conditioners to enhance your garden's health and productivity.

18. Can you assist with irrigation system installation?

Yes, we offer irrigation system design and installation services. Our team can assess your landscape's watering needs and recommend the most efficient system, whether it's drip irrigation, sprinklers, or a combination. Proper irrigation is crucial for maintaining a healthy garden, especially during dry spells.

19. What is your policy on cancellations or rescheduling?

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees. We strive to accommodate your schedule and will work with you to find a new time that suits your needs.

20. How do I stay updated on your services and promotions?

To stay informed about our services, promotions, and upcoming events, we recommend signing up for our newsletter on our website. You can also follow us on social media platforms for regular updates, gardening tips, and inspiration. We love engaging with our community and sharing our passion for gardening!
