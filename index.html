About Greenquill Garden

Our Botanical Narrative

Inspired by the quiet poetry of growing things, Greenquill Garden was founded by literary scholar-turned-horticulturist Eleanor Green in 2008. What began as a small consultancy blending garden design with narrative theory has blossomed into a full-service practice that approaches each landscape as a living manuscript awaiting its perfect composition.

We specialize in creating gardens that unfold like well-crafted prose—with careful attention to rhythm, contrast, and emotional resonance between spaces.

Gardening Tips

Simple advice to nurture your green space

Know Your Soil

Understanding your soil type is crucial for selecting the right plants and ensuring their health.

Water Wisely

Water early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize evaporation and ensure deep root hydration.

Embrace Native Plants

Native plants are adapted to your local climate and soil, making them easier to care for and more beneficial to local wildlife.
